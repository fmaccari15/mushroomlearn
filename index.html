About me

I am a second-year Manufacturing and Design Engineering student at Northwestern University pursuing a minor in Art Theory and Practice. These areas challenge and complement each other, giving me the tools to trace product design, creative, communication, and geometry-oriented careers.

My passions are also presented as a member of Mayfest Productions' Promotion Committee, the organization behind the nation’s largest student-run music festival, and as a member of NU BAJA SAE, a student team that designs and builds a car for off-road competitions. This winter, I am at San Francisco as a part of Northwestern’s Bay Area Immersion Program, delving more into the studies of the intersection of design and media innovation.
